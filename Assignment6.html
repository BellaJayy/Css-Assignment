FAQs

How does URL shortening work?      -

URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a shorter, condensed version that reflects to the original URL. When a user clicks on the shortened link, they are redirected to the intended destination

Is it necessary to create an account to use the URL shortening service?      +

Yes, you need to create an account to use the URL shortening service. This allows you to track and manage your shortened URLs, as well as access additional features such as custom URLs and analytics

Are they shortened links permanent? Will they expire?      +

Shortened links are permanent and will not expire. However, you can choose to delete or edit your shortened links at any time.

Are there any limitations on the number of URLs I can shorten?      +

There are no limitations on the number of URLs you can shorten. You can shorten as many URLs as you need, depending on your account type

Can i customize my shortened URLs to reflect my brand or content?     +

Yes, you can customize your shortened URLs to reflect your brand or content. You can create custom slugs, branded links, and domain customization options to reinforce your brand presence and enhance user engagement

How can I track the performance of my shortened URLs?      +

You can track the performance of your shortened URLs by accessing the analytics dashboard. This dashboard provides insights into the number of clicks, geographic location of users, and other key metrics to help you optimize your online experience

How secure is the URL shortening service? Are the shortened links protected against spam or malicious activity?      +

The URL shortening service is secure and protected against spam or malicious activity. We use advanced security measures to ensure that your shortened links are safe and secure. Additionally, we monitor and block any suspicious activity to protect your links and data

What is a QR code and what can it do?      +

A QR code is a two-dimensional barcode that can be scanned by a smartphone or QR code reader to access information, websites, or other digital content. QR codes can be used to share links, contact information, and other data quickly and easily

Is there an API available for integrating the URL shortening service into my own application or website?      +

Yes, there is an API available for integrating the URL shortening service into your own application or website. The API allows you to create, manage, and track shortened URLs programmatically, as well as access additional features and functionality
